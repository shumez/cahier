### 4.8 sCCA

- $\mathbf{X}_{n \times p}$, $\mathbf{Y}_{n \times q}$: mat
- $n$: num of obs (participants)
- $p$, $q$: num of var (feat)
- $\mathbf{u}$, $\mathbf{v}$ 

$$\begin{align*}
\tag{1}
\text{maximize } \mathbf{u}^T\mathbf{X}^T\mathbf{Yv}, \text{subject to } ||\mathbf{u}||^2_2 ≤ 1, ||\mathbf{v}||^2_2 ≤ 1, \\
||\mathbf{u}||_1 ≤ c_1, ||\mathbf{v}||_1 ≤ c_2
\end{align*}$$
