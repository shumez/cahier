## 1. Intro


"scale":

1. *spatial*
	- cell /synapse
	- brain region / large-scale fiber tract
2. *temporal*
	- sub-millisecond
	- entire lifespan / evolutionary changes across different species
3. *topological*
	- individual nodes
	- network
